HOW IT WORKS

Craving a shawarma from Tantra? Just place your order on the app and someone currently at the canteen can deliver it to your room for some extra fee.Getting started with IIITEats is easy. Simply create an account, and set up your profile. Once you're all set up, you're ready to start browsing through menus of any of the canteens and placing orders. To order, you can simply place an order from not just one, but multiple canteens and get your food delivered to you in minutes.To deliver, you can conveniently accept an order from the nearest canteen, pick up some food, and earn some extra cash!
